"hello world" speaking map

"hello world" speaking map

as a linguaphile, i am forever fascinated by foreign languages and their distinct alphabets, grammar, and pronunciation. in this pet project, i wanted to see if countries that are physically close to each other had similar-sounding languages, and used a map as the medium to visualize and explore proximity and language.

instructions:
please turn your volume on! click on a country in the sidebar or hover over a blue marker to hear and see how "hello world" is said and written in the country's main language.
